How to Cook Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) of tomato paste
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) of tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil leaves
  • 2 cups of ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2/3 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley
  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, sugar, salt, and basil. Simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, 1 1/2 cups of mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, and parsley.
  5. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  6. Arrange 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  7. Spread one-third of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  8. Spoon one-third of the meat sauce over the cheese mixture.
  9. Repeat layers twice, ending with meat sauce on top.
  10. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese on top.
  11. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the lasagna is hot and bubbly.
  13. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
